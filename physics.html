Orbital Mechanics

The vis-viva equation relates the velocity of an orbiting body to its distance from the central body:

v = √(μ (2/r - 1/a))

Where v is the velocity, μ is the standard gravitational parameter, r is the radial distance, and a is the semi-major axis.
